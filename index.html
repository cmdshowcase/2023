2023 Student Showcase:
UX Design Documentation

From March to June 2023, our UX 2 class embarked on an exciting journey to explore the depths of user experience design. Through hands-on projects and practical applications, students gained invaluable insights into creating seamless and engaging experiences for event and advertisement campaigns. The ultimate goal of the course was to prepare a showcase of student projects that would exhibit their talent, creativity, and skills gained throughout the semester.
